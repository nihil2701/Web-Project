Melon and kiwi smootie

Ingredients:

  • 1 kiwi, cut
  • 2-3 slices of papaya
  • 2 slices of melon
  • A couple of grapes
  • 1 plum, sliced
  • 1 glass milk
  • 1/2 - 1 tsp honey
  • 1/2 cup oats

Method:

  1. Mix all the ingredients in a blender and blend well.
  2. Serve chilled.
  3. You can use sugar/honey according to your taste.
  4. Fell free to throw in any fruits that you like.
